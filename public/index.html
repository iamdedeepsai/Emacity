Our vision

Ever since the start of the 20th century, humanity has been making advancements in the fields of science and technology, both to cater to world needs and the satisfaction of consumers. As giants in industries pass the baton to the next generation, the world has started to starve in search for the next batch of harbingers. But as technological advancements occur, it has now become all the more imperative to have everybody in the world on the same page, including the 3rd world. Air and water insecurity has become the elephant in the room for the United Nations and other non-profit organisations. Diseases such as Malaria, Cholera, Typhoid etc have destroyed societies and tormented multiple nations. Cholera affects approximately 2.9 million people yearly, leading to 95,000 deaths worldwide mainly in low- and middle-income countries. In Africa alone, 40 million people live in cholera-endemic areas with a risk of frequent outbreaks. We thus want to find a way to reach out to these rural and undeveloped corners of the world and aid them in getting the same privilege as the average urban individual.
